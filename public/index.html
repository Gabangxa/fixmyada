FAQ

Common questions

Will fixing the issues in your report make me legally safe?
Automated scanning catches ~57% of WCAG violations. Our report documents your effort to remediate issues and can demonstrate good-faith compliance to opposing counsel. We recommend pairing with a manual audit for any active litigation. We are not legal counsel.
Is an overlay widget like AccessiBe a valid alternative?
No. The FTC fined AccessiBe $1M in January 2025 for deceptive compliance claims. Overlay widgets cannot fix server-rendered markup. 22.6% of ADA lawsuits in H1 2025 targeted sites already running an overlay. Code fixes are the only defensible approach.
Do I need a developer to use this?
Not for the scan. You'll need a developer (or your webmaster) to apply the code fixes — but we give them the exact HTML changes required. Most developers can implement a full FixMyADA report in 2–4 hours.
What WCAG standard do you test against?
WCAG 2.2 AA — the standard codified by the DOJ's April 2024 Final Rule and required by ADA Title III case law in the 9th and 11th Circuits.
